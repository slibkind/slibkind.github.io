Sprites in Scratch

In this video we start our first Scratch program.

Assignment

  1. Create your own Scratch project
  2. Add at least one sprite to your project
  3. Optional: Change the stage backdrop

Looks in Scratch

In this video, we learn how to change what the sprites look like.

You can explore the project in this video here.

Assignment

Complete two of the following challenges

  • Write a script that changes the color of the sprite
  • Write a script that makes your sprite grow and shrink
  • Write a script that makes your sprite disappear and reappear
  • Add another costume to your sprite, and write a script that changes the costume

Motion in Scratch

In this video, we learn how to make the sprites move around the stage.

You can explore the project in this video here.

Assignment

Complete one of the following challenges

  • Write a script that makes your sprite walk back and forth across your screen
  • Write a script that makes your sprite bounce
  • Write a script that makes your sprite rotate in a circle

Further Exploration

  • Add sound to your Scratch project by using blocks from the "Sound" menu
  • Add more sprites to your project
  • Make two sprites have a conversation
  • Make the stage backdrop change during the animation
  • For more ideas:
    • Explore other Scratch projects
    • Work with your classmates
